# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Trader Bias ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our website.

## Information We Collect

### Information We Do NOT Collect

Trader Bias does **not**:
- Require user accounts or registration
- Collect personal identification information
- Store your trading data
- Track your trading activity
- Sell any data to third parties

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain information may be automatically collected:

**Server Logs**: Standard web server logs may record:
- IP addresses (may be anonymized)
- Browser type
- Referring pages
- Date/time of visits

**Cloudflare**: Our website uses Cloudflare for security and performance. Cloudflare may collect certain technical information as described in their privacy policy: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/

### Local Storage

We may use your browser's local storage to save:
- User preferences (e.g., notification settings, selected thresholds)
- Session data

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

## Third-Party Services

### Data APIs

We fetch data from third-party APIs including:
- Hyperliquid
- Binance
- Bybit
- Other cryptocurrency exchanges

These requests are made directly from your browser to these services. Please review their respective privacy policies.

### No Analytics (Currently)

We do not currently use analytics services like Google Analytics. If this changes, this policy will be updated.

## Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking purposes. Cloudflare may set necessary cookies for security purposes.

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect against unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## Children's Privacy

This Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data, including:
- Right to access
- Right to deletion
- Right to opt-out

Since we collect minimal data, most of these rights are automatically satisfied.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
